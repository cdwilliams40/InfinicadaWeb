Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Infinicada ("I", "me", or "my") handles information when you use the Focus Pocus mobile application (the "App").

The Short Version: I do not collect, store, share, or sell your personal data. Focus Pocus is designed to function entirely offline on your device.

1. Data Collection and Use

I adhere to a strict "Zero Data" policy:

  • No Personal Information: The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, or phone number.
  • No Usage Data: The App does not track how long you use it, which apps you block, or when you block them. All configuration settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: The App does not contain third-party tracking tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Facebook Pixel).

2. Android Permissions

To function as a productivity tool, Focus Pocus requires specific permissions from your Android device. These permissions are used strictly for functionality and never for data collection.

  • NFC (Near Field Communication): Required to detect when you tap your phone against an NFC tag to trigger blocking or unblocking. This data is processed locally in real-time.
  • Usage Access / App Overlay: Required to detect when a blocked app is opened so that Focus Pocus can intervene and show the blocking screen. This monitoring happens locally on your device OS; no app usage history is uploaded.

3. Data Retention

Since I do not collect any data, I have no data to retain. If you uninstall the App, all local settings and configurations are immediately deleted from your device.

4. Advertising

Focus Pocus is free and does not display third-party advertisements. Therefore, no ad-targeting data is collected.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. As the App does not collect personal information, I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Me

If you have any questions or suggestions about my Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me.
