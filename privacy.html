Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Infinicada ("I", "me", or "my") handles information when you use the Focus Pocus mobile application (the "App").

The Short Version: I do not collect, store, share, or sell your personal data. Focus Pocus is designed to function entirely offline on your device.

1. Data Collection and Use

I adhere to a strict "Zero Data" policy:

  • No Personal Information: The App does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide your name, email address, or phone number.
  • No Usage Data: The App does not track how long you use it, which apps you block, or when you block them. All configuration settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: The App does not contain third-party tracking tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Facebook Pixel).

2. Android Permissions

To function as a productivity tool, Focus Pocus requires specific permissions from your Android device. These permissions are used strictly for functionality and never for data collection.

  • NFC (Near Field Communication): Required to detect when you tap your phone against an NFC tag to toggle focus on/off. Tag data is processed locally in real-time and never stored or transmitted.
  • Accessibility Service: Required to monitor which app is in the foreground so Focus Pocus can enforce blocking and time limits. This monitoring happens entirely on-device; no app activity is uploaded.
  • Notification Access: Required to cancel notifications from blocked apps during active focus sessions, preventing interruptions from slipping through.
  • Do Not Disturb Access: Required to optionally silence all device notifications during a focus session. Only activated if you choose this setting; all control stays on-device.
  • Usage Stats Access: Required to track per-app screen time for daily time limits and the Insights dashboard. Usage data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted.
  • Camera: Required to scan QR codes that activate saved focus presets. The camera is only active while the QR scanner is open and no images are stored.
  • Location & Wi-Fi State: Required to read the name (SSID) of the connected Wi-Fi network for the Smart Triggers feature (auto-activate focus on a specific network). Location data is used only to read the Wi-Fi SSID, is never stored, and is never transmitted.
  • Bluetooth: Required to detect connections to specific Bluetooth devices for the Smart Triggers feature (auto-activate focus when your headphones or desk speaker connects). No Bluetooth data is stored or transmitted.
  • Post Notifications: Required to display focus session status updates and schedule reminders in the system notification shade.
  • Receive Boot Completed: Required to restore active focus sessions and scheduled rituals after your device restarts, so blocking resumes automatically without manual intervention.
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep Wi-Fi network monitoring active in the background so Smart Triggers can detect network changes even when the app is not open.

3. Data Retention

Since I do not collect any data, I have no data to retain. If you uninstall the App, all local settings and configurations are immediately deleted from your device.

4. Advertising

Focus Pocus is free and does not display third-party advertisements. Therefore, no ad-targeting data is collected.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. As the App does not collect personal information, I do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. I will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Me

If you have any questions or suggestions about my Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact me.
