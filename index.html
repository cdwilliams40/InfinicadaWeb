Privacy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

Your data stays on your device.

Focus Pocus is built with a privacy-first philosophy. All app activity, blocking configurations, and usage stats are stored locally and never uploaded.

  • No Personal Data: No name, email, account, or personal information is ever collected.
  • No Advertising ID: The advertising identifier is explicitly removed — there is no cross-app tracking or ad targeting.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use Focus Pocus.
  • Permissions Stay Local: The app requires several Android permissions to power its features — all data stays on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Optional Anonymous Analytics: With your consent, the app sends anonymous crash reports and basic usage statistics to help fix bugs. No personal data is included. You can turn this off anytime in Settings.
