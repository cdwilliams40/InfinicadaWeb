Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 30, 2026

I do not want your data.

Focus Pocus is designed with a "Privacy Absolute" philosophy. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

  • No Data Collection: I do not collect, transmit, or store any usage data, personal information, or app activity.
  • No Tracking: There are no third-party analytics or tracking pixels installed in the app.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use Focus Pocus.
  • Permissions Stay Local: The app requires several Android permissions (Accessibility, NFC, Notifications, Location, Bluetooth, Camera, Usage Stats) to power its features — all data those permissions touch stays on your device and is never uploaded.
